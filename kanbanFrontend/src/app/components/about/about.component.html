What is kanbanboard ?

Kanban is a project management tool that allows you to get a more visual overview of the tasks that either need to get done or are complete. At its core, it consists of a physical or digital board with three columns (To Do, In Progress, Done) and tasks listed as story cards.

six rules for the effective application of Kanban:

1) Never pass on defective products
2) Take only what is needed
3) Produce the exact quantity required
4) Level the production
5) Fine-tune production
6) Stabilise and rationalise the process.

Structure of kanabanboard

The basic structure of the Kanban board online has three columns: to-do, in-progress, and done. Each column represents the stage of development from the beginning when ideas are created, to the work in progress, and then finally, to when the work has been completed.

Workflow

A Kanban workflow defines explicit policies and principles, followed by the Kanban team. Its main objective is to represent the rules and procedures of work while the work is flowing across different stages of its development and delivery cycle. Create a project and a board. Set and manage activities in the workflow. Choose online Kanban boards with cards to communicate statuses, progress, and issues. Add tasks, bugs, or user stories to the backlog.
